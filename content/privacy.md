## 1. Who We Are
**GC Education Analytics** ("we," "us") provides custom software solutions for schools. Our website address is: https://gceducationanalytics.com.

## 2. The Data We Collect
When you sign up for our pilot, join a waitlist, or schedule a consultation, we collect the following personal information via our forms (Tally/Google Forms) or scheduler (Calendly):
* Name
* School/District Name
* Email Address

## 3. How We Use Your Data
We use this data solely for the purpose of:
* Contacting you regarding your application for the Pilot Program.
* Scheduling onboarding calls.
* Sending you product updates or resources (only if you have opted in).

## 4. Student Data (FERPA Compliance)
Our software products, specifically the **School Analytics Dashboard**, are designed with a "Privacy-First" architecture:
* **Local Processing:** The software runs entirely within your school's Google Drive environment.
* **No Data Transfer:** GC Education Analytics **does not** collect, store, transmit, or have access to any student data or Personally Identifiable Information (PII) processed by the dashboard.
* **Ownership:** All student data remains the sole property and responsibility of the School/District.

## 5. Third-Party Tools
We use the following trusted third-party services to run our website and business operations:
* **GitHub Pages:** For website hosting.
* **Calendly:** For scheduling meetings.
* **Tally / Google Forms:** For collecting contact information.
